Yin Yoga and Daoism

Yin yoga is based on Daoist philosophy. The notion of the Dao (also called The Way) is a notion that comes from China, the notion of the universal law of being.

The Dao is what cultivates the life force. It is the world unfolding according to its own laws, ever changing, ebbing and flowing like the phases of the moon, the changes of seasons and the tides in the ocean in a never ending cycle. It recognises how opposite forces such as Yin and Yang are interconnected in the natural world and how they give rise to each other as they interrelate to one another.

Yang

  • Strengthening
  • Warming
  • Transforming
  • Achieving
  • Bright, vibrant and dynamic

Yin

  • Softening
  • Cooling and calming
  • Yielding
  • Accepting
  • Nourishing
